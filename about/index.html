About

I'm a web developer, I wrote code for a living and for fun. I live in East London, also for fun.

I like taking things apart, understanding how they work, and solving problems. Sometimes I write about these things on my blog.

My day job is Senior Frontend Developer at the Government Digital Service, but I also do full stack development. I've been professionally working on the web for over 10 years.

I like to visit geek events and gigs in London, so you might meet me at a conference, a hack-day or a barcamp (or at [Pub Standards](https://london.pubstandards.com/)).

If you like, there are photos of me on Flickr.
